Latest Important Judgments

    CRA-D-569-DB-2009--

    It has been reiterated that the Empowered Officer, under the provisions of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985, is not a Police Officer and the statement recorded by such an Officer, is not a statement made to a Police Officer and not inadmissible in evidence. The judgment in Noor Agha v. Customs, (2008)16 SCC 417 and some other judgments, have been explained relying upon the Constitution Bench judgment in Ramesh Chandra Mehta v. The State of West Bengal, AIR 1970 SC 940. It is also held that a complaint by the Narcotics Control Bureau is maintainable and it is not required to complete investigation under Chapter XII of the Code of Criminal Procedure by lodging an FIR.
